void projectlp2a.AI.makeTurn ( )

The turn of an ai.

The ai will roll the die, then choose a case. If she cannot do anything, she will struggle and then skip her turn. Else she will choose a Case by "thinking" with AiThoughts (no she does not dream of electric sheep). She will not stop until she is done.
